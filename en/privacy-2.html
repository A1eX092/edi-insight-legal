Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

No data collection

EDI Insight does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All analyzed files are processed exclusively on your device, locally.

Imported files

The XML files imported into the app are analyzed in memory and are never written to a remote server, nor shared with any third party.

Local history

Your analysis history is kept solely on your device through iOS local storage. You can erase it at any time from within the app.
